Information Regarding Soccer

History

According to Fédération Internationale de Football Association, more commonly known as FIFA, modern soccer began in 1963 in England, although the earliest forms of kicking a ball date back thousands of years back to China’s Han Dynasty.

Popularity

With Soccer being the official most popular sport in the world, it also results in major amount of viewers both online and in person. The World Cup, an international soccer tournament that takes place every 4 years, is the most popular and watched sporting event in the world, with the latest edition, the 2022 Qatar World Cup, totaling over 5 billion viewers in the final alone.

Globality

According to numerous reports and statistics, soccer is officially the most popular sport in the world, totaling over 3.5 billion fans worldwide. Additionally, it is the most played sport in Europe, South America, Africa, and Asia, meaning 4 of the 7 continents primary sport is soccer. Soccer has proliferated through all manner of country and culture, its the sport of choice for people all across the globe, and as such has attracted billions of streadfast fans.
